Hband

Core thought behind the product is to enhance the ability of deaf and dumb to persive high pitch noises. Further the idea was to improve the product and add array of microphones and multipule vibration motors to help the user know the direction of sound

Introduction

The band has Atmega328P at its heart which controls the vibration motor and turns it on depending upon the noise level. The senstivity of system can be adjustied by pushing the button.There are 3 senstivity levels. 3 leds display the current senstivity level. Future improvements can be ability to detect the direction of sound and differentiate between different sounds
